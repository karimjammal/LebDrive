Question Only Test

This is a question-only test, it consists of 30 questions. You will not be asked on signs. You will have exactly 15 minutes to submit. Once you are done, you will be able to check the correct answers. You must score above 24 (i.e 25/30) in order to pass the exam.

Signs Only Test

This is a signs-only test. You will only be asked on the significance of 30 random signs. You will have exactly 15 minutes to submit. Once you are done, you will be able to check the correct answers. You must score above 24 (i.e 25/30) in order to pass the exam.

Full Test

Once you are ready, you can start with the full test. This is the official exam. It consists of 20 random law questions as well as 10 random signs questions. You have exactly 15 minutes to submit. You must score above 24 (i.e 25/30) in order to pass. Good luck!
Note: you will not be able to check the correct answers once you submit.

Things To Keep In Mind

Here are some things to keep in mind before taking your exam:

  • Do no overthink the questions.
  • Read the questions one night before the exam.
  • On the day of the exam, make sure you have a good breakfast; a Zaatar sandwich for example. It will improve your concentration during the exam
  • The exam is not hard, but you need to know every question since some of them are not trivial, especially questions related to the law.
  • Practice as much as you can. It is the only way you can pass the exam
